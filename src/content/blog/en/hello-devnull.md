## Why another blog?

If you're reading this, congratulations — you've found the one place on the internet that proudly names itself after Unix's garbage disposal.

Every engineer I know has a graveyard of abandoned blogs. Ghost, Hugo, Jekyll, WordPress, Medium, Substack, hand-rolled React monstrosities. I've tried most of them. They all end the same way: three posts, six months of silence, a domain renewal you forgot to cancel.

## What's different this time?

Nothing, probably. But I'm older now, and I've accepted that the act of writing is the point — not the streak, not the audience, not the SEO.

## What to expect

- **AI & Data Engineering** — the stuff I do for a living
- **Startups** — the ones that burned and the lessons they left
- **Engineering culture** — hiring, teams, the human side of tech
- **Random deep-dives** — whatever catches my attention

## The stack

This site is built with [Astro](https://astro.build), deployed on [Cloudflare Pages](https://pages.cloudflare.com/), and styled to look like a terminal because I have a monospace font addiction.

No JavaScript frameworks were harmed in the making of this blog. Just markdown, static HTML, and vibes.

```bash
$ echo "hello, world" > /dev/null
# and yet, here we are
```
